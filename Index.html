World Leaders Meet for Climate Summit


10 Nov, 2025

In his opening remarks at the climate summit plenary session, President Costa emphasized the critical timing of COP30 in the fight against climate change and the need for multilateral action to face the climate emergency. COP30 is taking place at a decisive moment in our fight against climate change. The window of opportunity to act and avoid irreversible impacts on humanity and nature is closing fast. The existential threat posed by climate change in particular impels the European Union’s unwavering commitment to the Paris Agreement. -President Costa President Costa underlined the EU's commitment to clear climate goals and solidarity with those most at risk. As net greenhouse gas emissions fell by 37% between 1990 and 2023 in the EU - despite the economy growing by 68% - the EU remains convinced that this is the right path to reducing emissions by 55% by 2030 and achieving climate neutrality by 2050.

Back to Back champions

11 Nov, 2025

France came agonisingly close to joining an elite two-nation club when they were beaten in perhaps the greatest FIFA World Cup™ final of all time at Qatar 2022. Argentina’s penalty shootout victory prevented Les Bleus retaining the title they won at Russia 2018 – and joining Italy and Brazil as the only teams to have won back-to-back World Cups. Unstoppable Azzurri The Italians were the first to achieve the feat. Having lifted the trophy on their home turf in 1934, beating Czechoslovakia in the final, they headed to France 1938 in ominous form. Under the leadership of legendary coach Vittorio Pozzo, they were in the midst of an unbeaten run which eventually ran to 30 matches and spanned four years from 1935 to 1939. That sequence included a triumphant Olympic Football Tournament campaign in 1936 and the successful defence of their World Cup crown two years later. Italy overcame Norway, France and Brazil on their way to the championship match, where they beat Hungary 4-2 in a thrilling clash.

New Smartphone Sets Record Sales

12 Nov, 2025

Popular smartphone brand HONOR launched their new handset, HONOR X9B with a grand celebration at Bashundhara City Shopping Complex on Sunday (21 January).

The event included the grand opening of the HONOR Experience Center and a photo session with the famous tech YouTuber Samzone, reads a press release. During the event, several key figures like Mohammad Zahirul Islam, Managing Director of Honor Bangladesh's only Distributor Smart Technologies (BD) Limited, Country Director of Honor Bangladesh, Lang, Deputy Country Director of Honor Bangladesh, Muzahidul Islam, Head of Business (Honor Bangladesh) Abdullah Al Mamun, GTM Manager (Honor Bangladesh) Shuvonker Golder were present. Tech-savvy customers can easily purchase the smartphone online, enhancing their lifestyle experience. It's a sleek canvas for your dreams, crafted to empower every connection, capture every moment, and fuel your ambitions. Embrace the X9b, embrace the world without limits." Regarding the new smartphone in the market, the HONOR Bangladesh Office mentioned that the HONOR X9B, with the tagline "Unlock Your Extra Power," features outstanding and powerful specifications in the 5G category.
